RECIPE FOR TUNISIAN SHORBA

Ingredients:

~1lb meat or chicken cut up into small pieces

6-8 tbsp vegetable oil

10-12 gloves garlic

4 tbs ras el hanout spice

1 of a large or one small onion chopped 

2 tsp salt 

1 tsp black pepper

2 tbsp paprika

1/2-1 tsp cayenne

2 cup frik (or 'freekeh', green wheat)

1 cup celery chopped

1 cup cilantro chopped

one 12oz can tomato paste

2 tsp dry mint

1 cup chickpeas  (optional)

Water (amount will vary, have a pot or kettle of hot water ready during cooking)
