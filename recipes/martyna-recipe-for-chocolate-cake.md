# Chocolate cake recipe
This cake is a delicious, rich, chocolate sponge and nobody can resist it!

## Ingredients

- 200g flour
- 30g cocoa powder
- 3 free range eggs
- 100g softened butter
- 85g caster sugar

## Instructions

1. Mix everything together
2. Bake for 40mins at 200 degrees Celsius
3. Let cool and enjoy!
